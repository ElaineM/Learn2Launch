Got an idea?

How do you know if your idea has potential? Valiate your idea(s) with feedback from potential users, estimating potential market size, and figuring out technical feasibility. Craft a product concept statement, validate it with at least 8 potential users (make sure they are not people you know), and perform feasibility tests by identifying 10 quick solutions. This process will help you to crystalize your idea into a MVP that can be designed and prototyped in the next phase.

How does it work?

You may go thru multiple rounds of ideas and pivots. Don't afraid to go back to the drawing-board. That is just part of the process. Stay with it! Ideation is the process of generating new ideas or concepts in order to solve a specific problem. Brainstorming is an excellent way to explore alternatives, possibilities or choices as they relate to the problem. Use the tools we have: Persona will help you clarify your target users. Market size calculation will help you figure out the size of opportunity. And lastly, we provided you with the basic tools to become familiar with the digital technologies to get things moving on the technology front.
Good luck and let the fun begin!
